FSAE Scales Summary - May 2026

Published

2026-05-13 16:12:42

Overview

A total of 20 teams were scaled before Design. Of the cars, the lightest was 167.4kg (Univ of North Carolina - Charlotte), the mean car weight was 205.9kg, and the median car was 202.1kg.

For comparison, the lightest car in 2025 was 167.8kg (University of California - Berkley), the median car was 215kg, and the average was 220.9kg.

In 2024, the lightest was 162.8kg (Vanderbilt), the median 210.9kg, and the average was 214.2kg.

In 2023, the lightest was 160.6kg (Rose-Hulman), the median 213.4kg, and the average was 216.7kg.
